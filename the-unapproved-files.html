From the Field

Why This Section Exists

Solar installations are documented for permits, not for posterity. The paperwork captures what passed, not what was found. The photos show the finished job, not the condition it was in when the crew arrived. The final inspection record says approved — it doesn't note the three things that were fixed the morning of the inspection.

This section is the difference between what got approved and what actually happened. It is not a collection of complaints. It is a collection of evidence — from 13 years of being present on job sites where the distance between what was designed and what was built was visible, measurable, and in some cases significant.

Nothing here is fabricated. The names don't matter. The patterns do.
