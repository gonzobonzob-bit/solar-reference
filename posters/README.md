# Posters

Printable and screen-ready visual references for field and classroom use.

## Subfolders

- **system-overview/** — Full system topology diagrams: grid-tied, battery backup, and hybrid configurations. Includes AC and DC circuit boundaries.
- **wiring-diagrams/** — Component-level wiring layouts for combiners, inverters, disconnects, and service connections.
- **safety-reference/** — Arc flash hazard boundaries, lockout/tagout procedures, emergency de-energization steps, and PPE requirements.

## Format Notes

- Source files are in SVG where possible for easy editing and scaling.
- PDF exports sized for US Letter (8.5×11) and ANSI D (22×34) field prints.
- PNGs for quick on-screen reference on phones and tablets.
