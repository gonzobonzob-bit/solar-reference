400A Service — Important Limitations

A 400A residential service is almost always two independent 200A panels — not a single 400A bus. This is a manufacturer equipment limitation, not a NEC requirement. NEC evaluates each panel independently. The 120% calc runs against each 200A panel separately.

Manufacturer Equipment Limits
  • Tesla Gateway: rated for 200A service maximum. One gateway per panel required.
  • Enphase IQ System Controller: one per panel.
  • QCells Q.HOME CORE: designed for single 200A interconnection point.
Whole Home Backup Reality

True whole-home backup on a 400A split service requires ESS equipment on both panels — two complete independent systems. Most installers back up one panel and place critical loads there.

Communication & Isolation Warning

During a grid outage each panel's ESS operates independently. The systems do not communicate with each other natively. If one system depletes before the other, that half of the home loses power while the other continues running. Homeowners and sales representatives frequently overlook this limitation when discussing whole-home backup on 400A services. A full outage scenario discussion with the customer is required before system design.
