# NEC Reference

Quick-access summaries of National Electrical Code articles most relevant to solar PV design and installation.

References are based on **NEC 2020** unless otherwise noted. Sections that changed significantly in 2023 are flagged.

## Subfolders

| Folder | Article | Subject |
|---|---|---|
| `article-690/` | Art. 690 | Solar photovoltaic systems |
| `article-705/` | Art. 705 | Interconnected electric power production sources |
| `article-230/` | Art. 230 | Services (utility interconnection rules) |
| `code-tables/` | Various | Ampacity tables, correction/adjustment factors, conduit fill |

## Frequently Referenced Sections

- **690.7** — Maximum voltage
- **690.8** — Circuit sizing and current
- **690.12** — Rapid shutdown of PV systems on buildings
- **690.15** — Disconnecting means
- **705.12** — Point of connection (load-side tap, supply-side connection, bus bar rule)
- **230.82** — Equipment connected to the supply side of service disconnect
